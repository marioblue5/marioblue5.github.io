  • Internship Position 1

    Clearable Talent Pipeline (CTP) pilot program, which will be launching its second cohort in Spring 2025 and starting applications on September 2nd. The CTP is an initiative taken by our directors, Dr. Damon Woodard and Dr. Domenic Forte, to create a selective talent pool of high-performing undergraduate students interested in pursuing careers in the National Security community and provide them with knowledge and research experience with AI and Machine Learning and start them early on their paths to earning a Security Clearance.
